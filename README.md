## About

I received both my undergraduate and master's degrees from Sun Yat-sen University. I am currently a Research Engineer at Tencent.

My research interests include vision-language agents, multimodal reasoning, game AI, and image enhancement. Previously, I worked on low-light image enhancement, controllable image enhancement, face inpainting, and monocular depth estimation.
